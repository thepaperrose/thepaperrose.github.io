About Me

Hey! I'm Angi. I owned my own hospitality company when COVID came, and I used that time to explore a few options and ideas for my next adventure in life. After working in the hospitality industry for 23 yrs, I sold my business and went back to school. I dabbled in HTML and CSS with SoloLearn, Codecademy, freeCodeCamp. I learned a bit of Data Science with IBM, but felt like there was a lot of gatekeeping, which I now know to be more along the lines of realism. Eventually, I found Gerry Auger and the Simply Cyber community. I felt like I had found my new family! Feel free to join me on my journey, and please leave constructive feedback.
